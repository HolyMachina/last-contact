# Characters

This character compendium has three parts.

- [Age and Species Comparison](#age-and-species-comparison) contains a chart speaking to each species each major character is, when they were born and what age they were in certain key years. You can click on each character's name in the table to be taken to their description.
- [Brief Character Descriptions](#brief-character-descriptions) has a description of each major character, their physical description and some key traits involved with their character and their state as of January 1, 2285 (the start of the core story)
- [Character Location Timeline](#character-location-timeline) is a set of charts that shows which orbital body each character is at or near during different parts of the story.

## Brief Character Descriptions

### Humans

#### Timothy Li

- **Born:** Taito City, Tokyo, Terra Firma Empire
- **Pronouns:** he/him/his
- **January 1, 2285:** 20, political prisoner, lost boy, trusting.
- **Physical Description:** Usually dressed in casual clothing, Tim has dark brown hair, brown eyes and olive skin.

#### Samuel Knudsen

- **Born:** Denver, Colorado, Terra Firma Empire
- **Pronouns:** he/him/his
- **January 1, 2285:** 23, political prisoner, unaware, living each day separately.
- **Physical Description:** Usually dressed in dark clothes, Sam, keeps his black hair long over one of his blue eyes.

#### Gwendolyn Romulus

- **Born:** Avalon Castle, Avalon, Planet of Venus, Republic of Venus
- **Pronouns:** she/her
- **January 1, 2285:** 21, Princess of Venus, political prisoner, dedicated, unfocused, unwilling.
- **Physical Description:** Usually wearing looser fitting clothing with long auburn hair and blue eyes she carries herself with an air of nobility.

#### Jamie Hedlund

- **Born:** Planum Castle, Martian South Pole, Mars, Terra Firma Empire
- **Pronouns:** he/him/his
- **January 1, 2285:** 21, Princess of Venus, political prisoner, dedicated, unfocused, unwilling.
- **Physical Description:** Usually wearing looser fitting clothing with long blonde hair and blue eyes she carries herself with an air of nobility.

#### Cassandra Fulton

- **Born:** Midtown Manhattan, New York City, Terra Firma Empire
- **Pronouns:** she/her/hers
- **January 1, 2285:** 26, Daughter of Terra Firma's Chancellor, chaos agent, loner, strategic mind.
- **Physical Description:** Hair of purple so dark it looks black in low lighting. Black eyes. Chaotic clothing choices with little regard to how they look or how much they cover of her.

#### Serafín Vera'Cruz

- **Born:** León, Mexico, Terra Firma Empire
- **Pronouns:** he/him/his
- **January 1, 2285:** 32, Special Advisor to the Chancellor of Terra Firma, empathetic, straightforward, confident.
- **Physical Description:** Always wearing an impeccably tailored suit to fit expectations, but looking uncomfortable in them Serafín keeps his light brown hair cropped and his brown eyes open at all times.
