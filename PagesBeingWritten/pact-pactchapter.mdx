# The Pact

## July 30, 2295

### Calypso

#### Crest Cross City

Allorien stood amongst their home of crystal and glass. Tim stood agape next to him. "This place is amazing."

A semi-loud rumbling grabbed his attention and Tim looked down through the glass sidewalk as a crystalline train sped past below. "You can even see the subways!"

_It's not that special,_ Allorien responded.
