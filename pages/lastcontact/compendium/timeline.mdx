# Timeline

This is a timeline of events leading up to and placing the story of last contact in chronological order by listing each existing chapter and the events surrounding them.

## 20th Century (1900 - 1999)

### April 24, 1990 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Discovery](../thepact/discovery.mdx): *Allorien watches the launch of the Hubble Space Telescope and wishes it really could be used to discover what humanity needed to see.*

### April 19, 1995 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Beginning](../thepact/beginning.mdx): *Allorien watches an act of terrorism. Instructed not to interfere they are staggered by humanity's desire to fight each other.*

## 21st Century (2000 - 2099)

### October 29, 2019 CE

- [Act 02 Factions - Spillover](../factions/spillover.mdx): *Allorien changes the natural course of the universe and thinks a pandemic will help unite humanity against a common foe.*

### September 6, 2041 CE

- [Act 02 Factions - World War Three](../factions/worldwarthree.mdx): *Humanity fights itself, again, in the third world war. The Eurasian Empire defends the city of Mariupol.*

## 22nd Century (2100 - 2199)

### April 5, 2112 CE

- [Act 02 Factions - Fracture](../factions/fracture.mdx): *One third of humanity leaves the only planet home we had ever known, Earth, to start a new life somewhere else.*

## 23rd Century (2200 - 2299)

### August 20, 2273 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Blow Up](../thepact/blowup.mdx): *A scientist who melds two oft separate practices becomes frustrated. Chancellor Fulton punishes his top generals. Cassandra Fulton, well, is Cassandra.*

### December 1, 2274 CE

- [Act 00 Prologue - The Three](../prologue/thethree.mdx): *Chancellor Fulton gives an order to eliminate a threat to the Terra Firma Empire.*

### December 15, 2274 CE

- [Act 00 Prologue - The Three](../prologue/thethree.mdx): *The remnants of the Li family are tried for illegal use of magic.*

### February 14, 2275 CE

- [Act 02 Factions - Building](../factions/building.mdx): *Chancellor Fulton leads his generals in building a plan to intimidate Terra Further. Cassandra has her own feelings about the project.*

### February 10, 2276 CE

- [Act 00 Prologue - The Three](../prologue/thethree.mdx): *The Venusian princess is abducted from aboard the royal state liner.*

### June 19, 2278 CE

- [Act 00 Prologue - The Three](../prologue/thethree.mdx): *Samuel Knudsen is arrested for finally being himself.*

### July 21, 2278 CE

- [Act 00 Prologue - Mission](../prologue/mission.mdx): *Jaedon Beyar is assigned by the King of Venus to rescue his daughter, but there are a couple of other things to do first...*

### December 3, 2280 CE

- [Act 02 Factions - Bridge](../factions/bridge.mdx): *Prince Hedlund receives a back channel communication from Senator Pace, in an attempt to create a calmer future.*

### September 1, 2282 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - The Meeting](../thepact/themeeting.mdx): *Allestra is recruited to work for Venusian Special forces after she pulls of a daring heist in an act unexpectedly defecting from the Terra Firma Special forces.*

### May 12, 2285 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Memories](../thepact/memories.mdx): *Alestra Amalee sneaks into the underground storage vault of Terra Firma's Empire to steal her way to a new life.*

### May 13, 2285 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Hatred](../thepact/hatred.mdx): *Chancellor Fulton Reacts to the theft of the memory cores from the underground vault and plans for a new system of control.*

### July 27, 2285 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Rescue](../thepact/rescue.mdx): *Tim Li and Samuel Knudsen are rescued in a classic prison break... from Saturn's third moon.*

### July 28, 2285 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Breakthrough](../thepact/breakthrough.mdx): *Tim reaches out with his mind, and finds something not quite human.*

### July 30, 2285 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Adjudication](../thepact/adjudication.mdx): *Humanity is on trial. Fae and Allorien the two litigators are both charged with overseeing humanity. They have very differing opinions.*

### September 2, 2285 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Detection](../thepact/detection.mdx): *Tim Li learns we are not alone in the universe. It sparks just the littlest hope in the universe.*

### October 15, 2285 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Contact](../thepact/contact.mdx): *Tim and Allorien make contact. Although, it didn't go quite how Allorien had hoped.*

### October 17, 2285 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - Welcoming](../thepact/welcoming.mdx): *After Retreating from Venus' space protections moving to Venus' Second Lagrange Point, Allorien is asked to come aboard Edith.*

### July 30, 2295 CE

- [Act 01 The Pact - The Pact](../thepact/pactchapter.mdx): *Tim Li and Allorien head to the Crysix home world of Eblypso to sign a pact between the two species.*

## 24th Century (2300 - 2399)

### November 17, 2306 CE

- [Act 00 Prologue - Dedication](../prologue/dedication.mdx): *Tim Li, the President of Humanity, consults with his closest advisors before one of the most important speeches of his career.*
- [Act 00 Prologue - Acknowledgement](../prologue/acknowledgement.mdx): *Tim Li, the President of Humanity, gives a speech to all of humanity that may save, or end, his career.*
